Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how MobInspect collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: January 2024 | Effective Date: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MobInspect ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MobInspect (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including scan history, features used, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and error logs to improve our services
  • Log Data: Server logs that may include your IP address, device type, browser type, and pages visited

2.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Contact Information: Email address when you contact us for support or feedback
  • Feedback and Communications: Information you provide when you contact us, report issues, or request features
  • Survey Responses: Information you provide when participating in surveys or research

2.3 APK Analysis Data

When you scan an APK file, we collect:

  • APK Metadata: Package name, version, file size, and signature information
  • Permission Data: List of permissions requested by the scanned application
  • Component Information: Activities, services, receivers, and providers declared in the application
  • Security Findings: Results of our security analysis including detected threats and vulnerabilities

Important: All APK analysis is performed locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit the actual APK files or their contents to our servers. Scan results are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us unless you explicitly choose to share them.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about data we do NOT collect:

  • Personal files, photos, videos, or documents on your device
  • Contact lists or address books
  • SMS messages, call logs, or communication content
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Content of applications you scan (only metadata is analyzed)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Provide and Maintain the Service

  • Perform security scans and analysis on APK files
  • Generate security reports and risk assessments
  • Store scan history locally for your reference
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

3.2 Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Identify and fix bugs, errors, and technical issues
  • Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Train and improve our AI detection models (using anonymized, aggregated data only)

3.3 Communication

  • Send push notifications about scan completions (if enabled)
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Send important updates about the App or our services

3.4 Legal and Security

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and illegal activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Scan reports and history are saved in your device's local storage
  • App settings and preferences are stored locally
  • AI models run entirely on-device for offline functionality

4.2 Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted to our servers (if any) is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access any collected data
  • Secure Development: We follow secure coding practices and conduct regular security audits
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data that is necessary for the App's functionality

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary:

  • Local scan data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • Usage analytics are retained for up to 24 months
  • Support communications are retained for up to 3 years
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

5. Sharing of Information

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our App (e.g., analytics providers, crash reporting services). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5.3 Aggregated Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you personally for research, analysis, or improvement purposes. This data includes general statistics about threat detection rates and app usage patterns.

6. Third-Party Services

Our App may integrate with or use the following third-party services:

6.1 Analytics Services

  • Firebase Analytics: Used to understand app usage and improve user experience. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Crashlytics: Used to collect crash reports and improve app stability. Crashlytics Terms

6.2 Push Notifications

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Used to send push notifications about scan completions. Firebase Privacy

6.3 App Distribution

  • Google Play Store: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data practices.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information

7.2 App Permissions

You can control app permissions through your device settings:

  • Storage Access: Required to read APK files for scanning
  • Notifications: Can be disabled in device settings
  • Background Processing: Can be restricted in battery settings

7.3 Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual scan reports within the App
  • Clearing all App data through device settings
  • Uninstalling the App (removes all local data)
  • Contacting us to request deletion of any server-side data

7.4 Opt-Out Options

  • Disable analytics in App settings
  • Disable push notifications in device settings
  • Opt out of personalized features

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the relevant age without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at orko.pmo.bulipe@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

9. International Data Transfers

If you access our App from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a different country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

We ensure that any such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of the "sale" of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at orko.pmo.bulipe@gmail.com.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent, contractual necessity, or our legitimate interests
  • Data Subject Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal
  • Lodge Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Our legal bases for processing personal data include:

  • Consent: Where you have given us permission to process your data
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary to perform our services
  • Legitimate Interests: Where we have a legitimate business interest that doesn't override your rights
  • Legal Obligation: Where we need to comply with legal requirements

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

  • Obtain your consent where required by law

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
