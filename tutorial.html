Step by Step

The Scan Process

Follow these steps to scan any APK file for security threats

01

Select Your APK File

Launch MobInspect and tap the scan button on the home screen. You have three options to select an APK:

  • Browse Files: Navigate to an APK file stored on your device
  • Installed Apps: Select from apps already installed on your phone
  • Recent Downloads: Quick access to recently downloaded APK files
Pro Tip: You can scan any APK file regardless of where it came from - app stores, direct downloads, or shared files.
02

AI-Powered Analysis

Once you select an APK, MobInspect's 3-layer AI detection engine begins analyzing:

  • Machine Learning: Pattern recognition using neural networks
  • Rule-Based: Known malware signature matching
  • Heuristic: Behavioral analysis for unknown threats
Background Scanning: Minimize the app while scanning - you'll get a notification when complete!
03

Review Security Results

After the scan completes, you'll see a comprehensive security report:

  • Score Tab: Overall risk score (0-100) with color-coded indicator
  • AI Analysis: Detailed scores from all three detection layers
  • Permissions: Analysis of requested permissions and risks
  • Malware: Detected threats and suspicious indicators
  • Privacy: Privacy leaks and tracker detection
04

Save Your Report

After reviewing the results, save the report for future reference:

  • Tap the "Save Report" button to store locally
  • Reports are saved in the app's Reports section
  • Access saved reports anytime from the bottom navigation
  • Reports include all scan details and findings
Privacy First: All reports are stored locally - never uploaded to any server.
Advanced Feature

Compare Reports

Compare security findings between different versions or apps

How to Compare Reports

  1. Open Reports Screen: Navigate to the Reports tab
  2. Enable Compare mode: Click the Icon on the Top-right corner
    Select Reports: Choose two reports to compare
       OR
    Alternate: Tap & Hold a report to select
    Select Another Reports: Choose second report to compare
  3. Tap Compare: Click the "Compare" button on the Top
  4. View Comparison: See side-by-side differences
