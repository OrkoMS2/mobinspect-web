Getting Started

How to Scan Your First APK

Step 1: Grant Permissions

When you first launch MobInspect, you'll need to grant the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: Required to read APK files on your device
  • Notification Permission: Optional, but recommended for scan completion alerts

IMAGE PROMPT: Android permission dialog for storage access with MobInspect branding. Dark theme, "Allow" button highlighted, app icon visible. Size: 400x300px

Step 2: Select an APK File

Tap the scan button on the home screen and choose how you want to select an APK:

  • Browse Files: Navigate to an APK file on your device
  • Installed Apps: Select from apps already installed on your device
  • Recent Downloads: Quick access to recently downloaded APK files

Step 3: Start the Scan

Once you've selected an APK, tap "Start Scan" to begin the analysis. The scan progress will show:

  • Current analysis stage (Permissions, Code Analysis, AI Detection)
  • Progress percentage
  • Estimated time remaining

Step 4: Review Results

After the scan completes, you'll see a comprehensive security report including:

  • Risk Score: Overall security rating from 0-100
  • Threat Summary: Any detected malware or suspicious behaviors
  • Permission Analysis: Breakdown of app permissions and their risks
  • AI Detection Results: Scores from all three detection layers
  • Recommendations: Actionable suggestions based on findings

Understanding Scan Results

Risk Score Interpretation

  • 0-30 (Low Risk): App appears safe. No significant threats detected.
  • 31-60 (Medium Risk): Some concerns found. Review the detailed findings.
  • 61-80 (High Risk): Significant issues detected. Exercise caution.
  • 81-100 (Critical Risk): Severe threats found. Do not install this app.

AI Detection Layers

Our 3-layer detection system provides individual scores:

  • Machine Learning (ML) Score: Pattern-based detection using trained neural networks
  • Rule-Based Score: Matches against known malware signatures and patterns
  • Heuristic Score: Behavioral analysis for unknown/zero-day threats

Permission Risk Levels

  • Safe: Standard permissions with minimal privacy impact
  • Moderate: Permissions that could affect privacy if misused
  • Dangerous: High-risk permissions that require careful consideration

Managing Scan Reports

Saving Reports

After a scan completes, you can save the report for future reference:

  • Tap the "Save Report" button on the results screen
  • Reports are stored locally on your device
  • Access saved reports from the "Reports" tab in the app

Sharing Reports

Share scan results with others:

  • Tap the share icon on any report
  • Choose to share as text summary or full report
  • Share via messaging apps, email, or save to files

Deleting Reports

Remove reports you no longer need:

  • Swipe left on a report in the list to delete
  • Or use the delete button in report details
  • Deleted reports cannot be recovered

Privacy & Security

Absolutely. MobInspect is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All scanning happens locally on your device
  • APK files are never uploaded to any server
  • Scan results are stored only on your device
  • We don't collect personal information
  • No account or registration required

Storage permission is required to read APK files from your device's storage. Without this permission, MobInspect cannot access the files you want to scan. We only read the specific files you select and never access other files on your device.

We collect minimal, anonymized usage analytics to help improve the app. This includes:

  • App crashes and errors (to fix bugs)
  • Feature usage statistics (to improve the UI)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility)

You can disable analytics in the app settings. We never collect information about the apps you scan.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Scan Stuck or Not Starting

Symptoms: The scan progress bar doesn't move or the scan fails to start.

Solutions:

  • Ensure storage permission is granted in device settings
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check if the APK file is corrupted (try a different file)
  • Clear app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí MobInspect ‚Üí Clear Cache
  • Ensure your device has at least 500MB free storage

App Crashes During Scan

Symptoms: The app closes unexpectedly during scanning.

Solutions:

  • Update to the latest version of MobInspect
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Restart your device
  • Try scanning a smaller APK file first
  • Report the issue via Contact Us with the APK name

Notification Not Showing

Symptoms: You don't receive scan completion notifications.

Solutions:

  • Check notification permission: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí MobInspect ‚Üí Notifications
  • Ensure notifications are not blocked by Do Not Disturb mode
  • Check that battery optimization isn't killing background processes
  • Enable "Show notifications" in MobInspect settings

Storage Permission Denied

Symptoms: Cannot select files or "Permission denied" error.

Solutions:

  • Go to device Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí MobInspect ‚Üí Permissions
  • Enable Storage/Files permission
  • On Android 11+, you may need to grant "All files access"
  • Restart the app after granting permissions

High Battery Usage

Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when using MobInspect.

Solutions:

  • This is normal during active scans (AI processing is CPU-intensive)
  • Battery usage should return to normal after scan completion
  • Disable background scanning if you don't need it
  • Enable battery saver mode on your device

False Positives

Symptoms: A legitimate app is flagged as malware.

Solutions:

  • Review the detailed findings to understand the specific concerns
  • Check if the app is from a trusted source (official app store)
  • Report false positives to us via Contact Us so we can improve detection
  • Consider the app's reputation and reviews from other users
